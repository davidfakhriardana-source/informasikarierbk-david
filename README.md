# RIASEC Career Guidance Service

## Introduction
The RIASEC Career Guidance Service is designed to assist high school students in exploring potential career paths based on their interests and strengths. The service utilizes the RIASEC model, developed by psychologist John Holland, to help students understand their personality types and how they relate to various careers.

## What is RIASEC?
The RIASEC model categorizes careers into six broad themes:
- **Realistic (R)**: Preferences for working with tools, machines, or animals; enjoys outdoor work and physical activity.
- **Investigative (I)**: Enjoys research, science, and problem-solving; prefers working with ideas and theories.
- **Artistic (A)**: Values creativity and artistic expression; enjoys working in environments that allow for originality.
- **Social (S)**: Prefers helping others, participating in social activities; values relationships and community service.
- **Enterprising (E)**: Enjoys leading, persuading, and influencing others; often involved in business or sales.
- **Conventional (C)**: Prefers structured tasks and working with data; values organization and efficiency.

## Services Offered
- **Career Assessments**: Students complete assessments to identify their RIASEC profile, helping them to better understand their strengths and preferences.
- **Individual Counseling**: One-on-one sessions with career counselors to discuss assessment results and explore suitable career options.
- **Workshops and Seminars**: Interactive sessions focusing on various career fields, resume building, and job search strategies.
- **Resources and Guidance**: Access to materials and tools to aid in career exploration, including information on educational requirements and job market trends.

## How to Use the Service
1. **Sign Up**: Students can sign up for the service through the school’s career center or online portal.
2. **Complete Assessments**: Take the provided assessments to receive your RIASEC results.
3. **Schedule Counseling**: Book a session with a career counselor to discuss your results and next steps.
4. **Attend Workshops**: Participate in available workshops that align with your interests.
5. **Explore Resources**: Utilize the provided resources to research potential careers and educational paths.

## Conclusion
The RIASEC Career Guidance Service aims to empower students in making informed career choices by helping them understand their unique strengths and preferences. For more information, students can contact their school’s career center or visit our website.
